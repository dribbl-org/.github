# Dribbl

# 🎨 Dribbl.org

> **Draw · Deceive · Deduce**

Dribbl is a multiplayer drawing game with a hidden imposter twist. Every round, all players share the same secret word — except one. That player is the **Imposter**, and they have no idea what anyone is drawing.

---

## 🕹️ How It Works

**One player is secretly the Imposter.** They don't receive the word and must figure it out by watching everyone else draw.

**Players take turns drawing on a shared canvas.** Each person adds to the drawing while trying to represent the secret word. The Imposter must bluff their way through — imitating what they see without knowing what it means.

**After all turns, everyone votes.** Who do you think the Imposter is? Choose carefully.

- 🟢 **Imposter correctly caught** → The players win
- 🔴 **Wrong guess or a tie** → The Imposter wins

---

## ⚖️ The Balance

The real challenge isn't just drawing well — it's drawing *smart*.

Draw too clearly and the Imposter copies you perfectly. Draw too vaguely and your own teammates won't know what the word is. Finding that balance is what makes every round different.

---

## ✨ Features

- Public matchmaking and private invite rooms
- Configurable draw time, rounds, and number of imposters
- Real-time shared canvas with full undo/redo
- In-game chat
- Works on mobile and desktop
